--- ABOUT AFC ---
The African Freefire Community (AFC) is the premier Free Fire competitive platform for serious players across Africa.
Website: https://africanfreefirecommunity.com

Mission: To foster a competitive and supportive environment for Freefire players across Africa, promote esports, develop talent, and create opportunities for players to showcase their skills on a global stage.

Vision: A thriving African Freefire esports scene recognized worldwide for its talent, passion, and professionalism.

Values:
- Integrity: Upholding the highest standards of fair play and sportsmanship.
- Community: Fostering a supportive and inclusive environment for all players.
- Excellence: Striving for continuous improvement in all endeavors.
- Innovation: Embracing new technologies and strategies to advance the Freefire esports scene.
- Empowerment: Providing resources and opportunities for players to develop their skills.

Stats:
- 1,200+ Tournaments Held
- $500K+ Prize Pool Distributed
- Active players and registered teams (growing)

--- PLATFORM FEATURES ---
- Competitive Tournaments: High-stakes tournaments with prizes and recognition.

--- RULES & HANDBOOK SUMMARY ---
Full rules at: https://africanfreefirecommunity.com/rules

Player Eligibility:
- Must meet account, age (13+), and regional requirements.
- One account per player; multi-accounting is strictly prohibited.
- Players cannot be on multiple teams simultaneously.

Team Registration:
- Teams must register and submit rosters.
- Social media and contracts must be submitted.
- Players must be in the AFC player database.

Code of Conduct:
- No cheating, no harassment, no hate speech.
- No use of aimbots, ESP, macros, or unauthorized software.
- No bribery or match-fixing.
- Sportsmanship is mandatory at all times.

Game Evidence:
- All matches must be recorded and submitted as evidence.
- No pauses allowed during matches.
- Players must be ready before scheduled match time.

Ranking System:
- Monthly rankings updated for teams and players.
- Quarterly tiering system (Tier 1, Tier 2, etc.) based on tournament wins, kills, placements, prize money, and social media metrics.

Disciplinary Actions:
- Minor and major offenses handled with sanctions.
- One banned player = 2-month ban for entire team.
- Two banned players = 4-month team ban.
- Three banned players = 6-month team ban.
- Appeals are allowed after serving 1 month.

Slot Buying/Selling:
- Tournament slots can be bought and sold.
- AFC charges a transaction fee.
- Strict timeline for slot transfers.

Prize Distribution:
- Winners must provide accurate payout details and complete identity verification.
- AFC is not responsible for bank delays or incorrect user information.
