--- SCORING SYSTEM — FULL DETAIL ---

TOURNAMENT CLASSIFICATION (applied as a multiplier to all performance points):
- Tier 1: $1,000+ prize OR official Free Fire event → 2.0× multiplier
- Tier 2: $300–$999 OR LAN event → 1.5× multiplier
- Tier 3: Below $300 → 1.0× multiplier

PLACEMENT POINTS (per match, per team/player):
1st=12, 2nd=9, 3rd=8, 4th=7, 5th=6, 6th=5, 7th=4, 8th=3, 9th=2, 10th=1, 11th-12th=0

KILL COMPRESSION SCALE (raw cumulative kills → points):
0–50=3, 51–100=7, 101–200=12, 201–300=17, 301–500=23, 501–750=28,
751–1000=33, 1001–1500=38, 1501–2000=43, 2001–3000=50, 3001–5000=58, 5001+=65

PLACEMENT COMPRESSION SCALE (raw cumulative placement pts → points):
0–50=5, 51–100=10, 101–200=17, 201–300=24, 301–500=31, 501–750=38,
751–1000=44, 1001–1500=50, 1501–2000=56, 2001–3000=62, 3001+=70

MONTHLY TEAM RANKING FORMULA:
For each tournament that month:
  tournament_score = (compress(placements) + compress(kills)) × tier_multiplier
                   + win_bonus (Tier1=30, Tier2=20, Tier3=12)
                   + (5 × tier_multiplier) per finals appearance

Scrim points (capped at 30% of tournament total):
  scrim_pts = (placement_pts × 0.5) + (kill_pts × 0.5) + (wins × 3)
  counted_scrim_pts = min(scrim_pts, total_tournament_pts × 0.30)

Monthly score = total_tournament_pts + counted_scrim_pts

MONTHLY PLAYER RANKING:
- Personal kills (compressed, same scale): 3–65 pts
- MVP award: 5 pts each
- Finals appearance: 3 pts each
- Team tournament win: 5 pts
- Tournament participation: 1 pt per event
- Scrim kills: 0.5× kill compression value
- Scrim wins: 1 pt per win

QUARTERLY TIERING — same formula as monthly but over 3 months, plus:
- Prize money points (₦ equivalent, see scale in ranking spec)
- Social media points: max 10 pts (Instagram + TikTok combined followers)
  0–1000=1, 1001–5000=3, 5001–10000=5, 10001–25000=7, 25001–50000=9, 50001+=10

Tier thresholds: Elite=150+, Tier1=90–149, Tier2=40–89, Tier3=below 40

Minimum tournaments for tier assignment: 2 per quarter (else defaults to Tier 3)

ANNUAL LEADERBOARD: sum of all 4 quarterly tier scores. Resets each January.

--- SCRIM ADMINISTRATION ---

Scrims master role ID: 1011438178630107207
Scrims masters can enter and verify scrim data for teams.

Key scrim rules:
- Daily cap per team: 4 scrims/day
- Monthly cap per team: 60 scrims/month
- Scrim contribution: max 30% of a team's tournament total for that period
- Scrims do NOT have a tier multiplier (unverified events)
- Teams cannot self-submit scrim data — admins/scrims masters enter all data
- Scrims against non-AFC registered teams count but must be admin-verified

Scrim scoring:
- Scrim placements: 0.5× tournament placement value
- Scrim kills: 0.5× kill compression value
- Scrim win bonus: 3 pts flat (no tier multiplier)

--- TRANSFER WINDOWS & ROSTER MANAGEMENT ---

Default schedule each quarter:
- Weeks 1–2: Transfer Window OPEN — roster changes allowed
- Week 3 onwards: Roster Lock — no changes until next quarter

Transfer window dates can be changed by Head Admin or Metrics Admin at any time.

Rules:
- A player can only be on ONE team per quarter
- Minimum 4 players per team to participate
- New teams joining after roster lock have their initial tournament roster locked in
- Late roster approvals require Head Admin or Metrics Admin sign-off
- Banned/dissolved team situations handled manually by AFC management

--- GHOST TEAMS ---

Ghost teams are provisional records for unregistered teams that compete in AFC events.
- Created by admins before entering match results
- Appear in tournament results but NOT on main leaderboard
- Do not contribute to ranked tier calculations until claimed
- When the real team registers, they submit a claim request
- Admin approves → all historical stats transfer to the registered team
- Historical scores are recalculated retroactively
- Original ghost record archived for audit trail
- Only one team can claim a ghost profile

--- USER / AUTH MANAGEMENT ---

Django app: afc_auth, URL prefix: /auth/

Key models:
- User (extends AbstractUser): role, profile, ban status
- SessionToken: manages login sessions
- UserProfile: extended profile data
- Roles + UserRoles: granular permission system
- TeamBan, BannedPlayer: ban tracking
- PasswordResetToken: 6-digit code, 10-minute TTL

Login accepts: email OR username
Password reset accepts: email OR Free Fire UID

Endpoints relevant to support:
- GET  /auth/get-user-profile/            — fetch user profile
- POST /auth/send-verification-token/     — trigger password reset
- POST /auth/ban-player/                  — ban a player (admin only)
- GET  /auth/is-discord-account-connected/ — check Discord link status
- POST /auth/connect-discord/             — initiate Discord OAuth

--- SHOP & PAYMENTS ---

Django app: afc_shop, URL prefix: /shop/
Payments processed via Paystack (reference stored on Order model).

Key models:
- Product → ProductVariant (has diamonds_amount, SKU, price)
- Cart → CartItem
- Order → OrderItem (Paystack reference on Order)
- Coupon: percent or fixed discount
- Fulfillment, Redemption

Tax rate: 7.5% applied client-side on the frontend.

Mintroute Diamond Shop (launching April 2026):
- Endpoint: GET /shop/mintroute/products/ — available packages
- Endpoint: POST /shop/mintroute/orders/ — place diamond order
- Endpoint: GET /shop/mintroute/orders/<id>/ — check order + pin code
- Rate limits: 10 purchases/min, 5 reservations/5-min
- Pin code delivered instantly on order completion
- All sales FINAL — no refunds once pin issued

--- DATA INTEGRITY RULES ---
- All tournament data entered by admins only
- Scrim data entered by admins/scrims masters — teams cannot self-submit
- Only AFC-sanctioned and admin-verified events contribute to scores
- Cheating/manipulation = full point reset for affected team and players
- Suspended/banned teams earn 0 points during suspension
- Prize money conversion rate locked at date of award (not date of entry)
- Ghost team stats transfer only once, only after admin approval

--- SUPPORT ESCALATION TRIGGERS ---
Escalate to support team for:
- Account bans, suspensions, lockouts
- Wrong Free Fire UID — needs admin correction in database
- Discord role not assigned after OAuth connection
- Payment or prize disputes
- Cheating reports and ban appeals
- Match results missing or wrong after 24 hours
- Private event invites needed from organiser
- Any action requiring admin direct intervention on platform
