--- SEASON 2026 STRUCTURE ---
AFC runs on a quarterly season system:
- Season 1 (Q1): January – March
- Season 2 (Q2): April – June
- Season 3 (Q3): July – September
- Season 4 (Q4): October – December

Rankings and tiers reset each quarter.
The Transfer Window is currently OPEN (March 2026) — the first 2 weeks of each quarter.
Roster Lock begins Week 3 of each quarter.

--- RANKING & TIERING SYSTEM ---

AFC uses 3 performance tracks:
1. Monthly Rankings — resets every month (short-term snapshot)
2. Quarterly Tiering — resets every quarter (assigns your official tier)
3. Annual Leaderboard — resets yearly (prestige; used for AFC Awards)

TEAM TIERS (assigned at end of each quarter):
- Elite: 150+ points
- Tier 1 (Competitive): 90–149 points
- Tier 2 (Rising): 40–89 points
- Tier 3 (Entry): below 40 points (default for inactive/new teams)

Teams must play at least 2 tournaments in a quarter to receive a tier.
Teams that don't meet the minimum default to Tier 3.
Teams must play at least 1 tournament in a month to appear on the monthly leaderboard.

PLAYER TIERS:
- If you are on a registered team, your tier = your team's tier
- If you have no team, your tier is calculated from your personal quarterly score

--- TOURNAMENT CLASSIFICATION ---
Every AFC tournament is classified by prize pool — this affects how many points you earn:
- Tier 1: $1,000+ prize pool OR official Free Fire event → 2× point multiplier
- Tier 2: $300–$999 OR LAN event → 1.5× point multiplier
- Tier 3: Below $300 → 1× point multiplier (base)

Win bonus (added on top of your tournament score):
- Win a Tier 1 event: +30 points
- Win a Tier 2 event: +20 points
- Win a Tier 3 event: +12 points

Finals appearance bonus: +5 × tier multiplier per appearance

--- HOW POINTS ARE CALCULATED ---

PLACEMENT POINTS (per match):
1st=12, 2nd=9, 3rd=8, 4th=7, 5th=6, 6th=5, 7th=4, 8th=3, 9th=2, 10th=1, 11th–12th=0

Points are COMPRESSED — raw cumulative totals are converted to a score:

Kill compression (total kills across all matches → score):
0–50=3, 51–100=7, 101–200=12, 201–300=17, 301–500=23, 501–750=28,
751–1000=33, 1001–1500=38, 1501–2000=43, 2001–3000=50, 3001–5000=58, 5001+=65

Placement compression (total raw placement pts → score):
0–50=5, 51–100=10, 101–200=17, 201–300=24, 301–500=31, 501–750=38,
751–1000=44, 1001–1500=50, 1501–2000=56, 2001–3000=62, 3001+=70

TEAM MONTHLY SCORE FORMULA:
For each tournament that month:
  score = (compress(placements) + compress(kills)) × tier_multiplier + win_bonus + finals_bonus

Scrim contribution (capped at 30% of your tournament total):
  scrim_pts = (placement_pts × 0.5) + (kill_pts × 0.5) + (wins × 3)
  max_scrim_pts = total_tournament_pts × 0.30
  counted_scrim_pts = min(scrim_pts, max_scrim_pts)

Monthly total = tournament_pts + counted_scrim_pts

PLAYER MONTHLY SCORE:
- Kills (compressed, same scale): 3–65 pts
- MVP award: +5 pts each
- Finals appearance: +3 pts each
- Team tournament win: +5 pts
- Tournament participation: +1 pt per event
- Scrim kills: 0.5× kill compression value
- Scrim wins: +1 pt each

QUARTERLY SCORE:
Same formula as monthly but summed over 3 months, plus:
- Prize money contribution (based on ₦ equivalent won)
- Social media bonus (max 10 pts combined from Instagram + TikTok followers):
  0–1,000=1pt, 1,001–5,000=3pts, 5,001–10,000=5pts, 10,001–25,000=7pts,
  25,001–50,000=9pts, 50,001+=10pts

ANNUAL LEADERBOARD:
Sum of all 4 quarterly scores. Resets each January. Used for AFC Awards.
