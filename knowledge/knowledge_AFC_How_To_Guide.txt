--- SECTION 1: GETTING STARTED ---

Step 1 — Visit the Website
Go to: https://africanfreefirecommunity.com
You'll land on the homepage where you can see the latest news, upcoming tournaments, and platform highlights.

Step 2 — Create Your Account
To participate in tournaments, join a team, or access the full platform, you need a free account.
1. Click the orange "Join Now" button in the top-right corner of the navigation bar.
2. Fill in your details on the registration page (username, email, password).
3. Verify your email address if prompted.
4. Log in using your new credentials.
TIP: Use a username that represents your gaming identity — this is how other players will know you across tournaments and leaderboards.

Step 3 — Log In
Already have an account?
1. Click "Log In" in the top-right corner of the navigation.
2. Enter your email and password.
3. Click "Login" to access your dashboard.

--- SECTION 2: NAVIGATING THE PLATFORM ---

The Navigation Menu gives quick access to all major sections:
- Home: Overview of the platform, stats, and featured content.
- Teams: Browse all registered teams, their rosters, and stats.
- News: Latest updates, announcements, and esports news from AFC.
- Awards: See who has earned badges, titles, and recognition.
- Tournaments: Browse all active, upcoming, and past tournaments.
- About Us: Learn about AFC's mission, vision, and values.
- Contact: Get in touch with the AFC team via email, Discord, or social media.

--- SECTION 3: HOW TO JOIN A TOURNAMENT ---

Step 1 — Go to the Tournaments Page
Visit: https://africanfreefirecommunity.com/tournaments
You'll see a list of all tournaments — both upcoming and completed.

Step 2 — Browse Tournaments
Each tournament card shows:
- Tournament name and banner image
- Date of the event
- Status: "Upcoming" or "Completed"

Step 3 — View Tournament Details
Click "View Tournament" on any card to read full details including rules, prize pool, format, and registration deadline.

Step 4 — Connect Your Discord Account (REQUIRED)
Before registering for any tournament, you MUST:
1. Join the official AFC Discord server: https://discord.gg/african-freefire-community-afc-920726990607237160
2. Connect your Discord account to your AFC profile by going to your Profile page and clicking "Connect" under Discord.
Without completing both steps, you CANNOT register for tournaments.
TIP: Connect your Discord account right after creating your AFC profile so you're always ready when a new tournament opens.

Step 5 — Register for the Tournament
- Make sure you're logged into your AFC account.
- Follow the registration instructions on the tournament detail page.
- For team tournaments, ensure your team is already created (see Section 4).
TIP: Check tournament dates early and register before the deadline. Spots can fill up fast, especially for high-prize events.

Tournament Status Types:
- Upcoming — Registration is open or opening soon. Sign up now!
- Completed — The tournament has ended. You can still view results and highlights.

--- SECTION 4: HOW TO CREATE & MANAGE A TEAM ---

Creating a Team:
1. Log into your account.
2. Navigate to the "Teams" section from the top navigation.
3. Click the "Create Team" button.
4. Enter your team name, tag/abbreviation, and any other required details.
5. Save your team profile.

Managing Your Team — Once created, you can:
- Invite players to join your roster
- Set player roles and positions
- Update team information and branding
- Register your team for upcoming tournaments
- Track your team's stats and performance

Joining an Existing Team:
1. Browse teams on the "Teams" page: https://africanfreefirecommunity.com/teams
2. Find a team that's recruiting players.
3. Send a join request or contact the team captain directly.
TIP: Build your team with players you trust and communicate well with. Good teamwork is just as important as individual skill in Free Fire.

--- SECTION 7: FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS ---

Q: Is AFC free to join?
A: Yes! Creating an account on AFC is completely free. Some tournaments may have entry requirements, so always check the details before registering.

Q: Do I need a team to participate?
A: Not always. AFC hosts both solo and team-based tournaments. Solo players can compete in individual events like the 'Detty December Solos' tournament. Check each tournament's format before signing up.

Q: How do I find out about new tournaments?
A: Check the Tournaments page regularly, follow AFC on social media, or join the AFC Discord server to get real-time notifications about upcoming events.

Q: What is AFC's fair play policy?
A: AFC is committed to fair competition. The platform uses anti-cheat measures and enforces strict fair play policies. Violations can result in disqualification or bans. See: https://africanfreefirecommunity.com/rules

Q: How are prizes distributed?
A: Prize distribution details are outlined in each tournament's details page. AFC has distributed over $500K in prizes across its tournaments.

Q: Can I report a player for cheating?
A: Yes. Contact the AFC team via email (info@africanfreefirecommunity.com) or Discord to report suspected cheating or unfair play.

Q: What regions does AFC serve?
A: AFC is focused on African players but welcomes the wider Free Fire community. The platform is designed to elevate African esports talent to the global stage.
